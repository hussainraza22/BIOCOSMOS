About Us

Project Biocosmos

Proposing Pakistan’s first institutionalized Biotech E-Magazine, serving as a potential for connecting scientists from different institutions of the nation on a single platform to participate in our publications by bringing forth their research work and ingenious discoveries. This project is intended to display the works being done in biotechnology thriving in the background in order to illuminate the scope of it amongst the readers and inspire the youth to develop a career trajectory. In addition to the research work collaboration yet another approach will be to incorporate analysis on the B-tech Entrepreneurship in works and the opportunities it holds for students to excel in their business skills.
The magazine would feature work done in the following disciplines:

  • • Biotechnology
  • • Molecular Biology
  • • Genetics
  • • Neurology
  • • Bioinformatics
  • • Biomedical Engineering
  • • Biosciences
BIOCOSMOS would include all data of biotechnological interlinks between these discipline

Contents of BIOCOSMOS include :

  • • News Column
  • • Comments/Opinion Column
  • • Career Info Column
  • • BIO-Research Column
  • • Q/A Column
  • • Project Column
  • • B-Tech Entrepreneurship Column
The Career Column serves info about the career journeys of our senior scientists in addition to information regarding the opportunities lying ahead for students nationally and internationally, scholarships, internships etc. The project Column is a space for especially students to promote their research projects whether they be related to health, agriculture, environmental biotechnology. The BIO-Research Column is categorized into 3 categories that’ll include research analysis/work done under BIO-Health, BIO-Agriculture, and BIO-Environmental. The B-Tech Entrepreneurship Column would introduce the readers to the current B-Tech startup and entrepreneurship culture.
